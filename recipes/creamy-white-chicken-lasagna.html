Ingredients

  • 3 Cups cooked and shredded chicken (Who actually measures chicken in cups? Shouldn't this be a weight measurement?)
  • 1 14 oz can of artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped (Again, why are we measuring dry ingredients in liquid form? Just get a scale and weigh it.)
  • 1 1/2 packages of low fat cream cheese
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 1 tsp minced garlic (2 or 3 cloves of garlic)
  • salt and pepper to taste (No shit. This shouldn't even need to be said)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped (Or how about 2 oz?)
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 12 slices whole milk mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In a large bowl, combine chicken, artichokes, 1 cup mozzarella, grated parmesan, 1/2 the basil and tomatoes. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl using an electric mixer, beat cream cheese, half and half, garlic, and salt and pepper to taste until well combined. Using a spoon, stir in half the basil. Set half of the cream cheese mixture aside and stir the remaining half in with the chicken mixture.
  4. Take half of the cream cheese mixture (half of the part without the chicken) and spread it into a 9×13 baking dish, covering the bottom. Lay 3 noodles on top and then top with 1/3 of the chicken mixture. Repeat the noodles and chicken mixture two more times, ending with a noodle layer.
  5. Top with the last half of the cream cheese mixture.
  6. Place the whole milk mozzarella slices on top in rows of three, like shown.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella over everything, and then top with more basil if desired.
  8. Bake 25 min. or until heated through.
  9. Slice and serve! Enjoy!
